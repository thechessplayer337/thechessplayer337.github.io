About Me

A short personal introduction behind The Chessplayer.

Hello, my friend!

I am a high school student from Shenzhen who is soon going to the University of California, Davis for my undergraduate journey. Feel free to contact me through email, whether for general communication or potential cooperation, and I will respond when I have time. Have a nice day!

Why did I create this website?

Chess and GeoGuessr are by far two of my favorite hobbies, and I deem myself relatively good at them among the general public. Thus, I wanted to create this website in order to record my own journey as well as share my experience to help people who share similar interests out there.

It is my great pleasure if you find my content helpful, and thank you for visiting!
