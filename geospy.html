Descriptions

Ready to vote? 60s

First choose Agree or Refuse, then press Confirm. Only confirmed choices count. The game moves on only when the outcome is decided: enough confirmed Agree votes start voting, or enough confirmed Refuse votes make voting impossible. If time runs out, unconfirmed players count as Refuse.

Vote out the spy 60s

Choose one living player, then press Confirm Vote. If time runs out, missing votes will be randomly assigned to another living player.
