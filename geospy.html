Ready to vote? 60s

First choose Agree or Refuse, then press Confirm. Only confirmed choices count. Once at least half of the living players have confirmed, the game checks whether confirmed Agree is at least half. If yes, voting begins; otherwise, a new description round begins. If time runs out, unconfirmed players count as Refuse.

Vote out the spy 60s

Choose one living player, then press Confirm Vote. If time runs out, missing votes will be randomly assigned to another living player.
